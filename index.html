Full Volume Worship

Full Volume Worship is so much more than cranking up the music in a high-energy service.

It is living a lifestyle of worship in such a way that others can hear and see the Gospel just by being around you.

So here’s what I want you to do, God helping you: Take your everyday, ordinary life—your sleeping, eating, going-to-work, and walking-around life—and place it before God as an offering.
Romans 12:1 (MSG)

We have a laid back "come as you are" atmosphere. Expect some high energy worship, a relevant and humorous delivery of the Word of God, and a few high-fives along the way.
